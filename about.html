About BD Bill Calculator

Empowering Bangladesh families to make informed decisions about their electricity consumption and save money through accurate, free bill calculations.

Our Mission

With rising electricity costs in Bangladesh, many families struggle to budget effectively for their monthly energy expenses. Our mission is simple: provide every household with a free, accurate, and easy-to-use electricity bill calculator that helps them understand their consumption patterns and find ways to save money.

We believe that financial transparency leads to better decisions. By knowing exactly how much each appliance costs to run, families can make informed choices about their energy usage and significantly reduce their monthly expenses.

Impact Goals

  • Help 100,000+ families save money
  • Reduce average bills by ৳500-800/month
  • Promote energy conservation awareness

Our Story

BD Bill Calculator was born from a personal experience that many Bangladesh families face every month - the shock of an unexpectedly high electricity bill. In early 2025, our founder Sowrav Hasan was helping his family understand why their DESCO electricity bill had suddenly increased by 40%.

After spending hours manually calculating the consumption of each appliance using paper and calculator, he realized there had to be a better way. Existing online calculators were either too generic for Bangladesh's unique slab-based pricing system or required payment to access full features.

That's when the idea for BD Bill Calculator was born - a completely free, comprehensive tool specifically designed for Bangladesh's electricity billing system, supporting all major utility providers including BPDB, DESCO, DPDC, and WZPDCL.

"Within just three months of using our own calculator, my family reduced their electricity bill by ৳750 per month simply by understanding which appliances were consuming the most energy and adjusting our usage patterns."

- Sowrav Hasan, Founder

Our Values

100% Free

We believe financial tools should be accessible to everyone, regardless of economic status. Our calculator will always remain completely free.

Accuracy First

We use official tariff rates from Bangladesh utility companies and update them regularly to ensure 95%+ accuracy in our calculations.

Privacy Protected

All calculations happen in your browser. We don't collect, store, or share any personal information about your electricity usage.

Our Commitment to Bangladesh

Supporting Local Families

We understand the unique challenges Bangladesh families face with rising electricity costs and frequent load shedding. Our calculator is specifically designed to account for these local conditions and help families adapt their energy usage accordingly.

Accurate Local Data

We maintain direct relationships with utility company representatives to ensure our rate data is always current and accurate. This includes regular updates for BPDB, DESCO, DPDC, WZPDCL, and other regional providers.

Continuous Improvement

User feedback drives our development. We regularly survey users to understand their needs and implement new features that make electricity bill planning easier and more effective for Bangladesh households.

Education Focus

Beyond calculations, we provide educational content about energy conservation, appliance efficiency, and practical tips specifically relevant to Bangladesh's climate and electrical infrastructure.
