Complete Guide: Reduce Your Bangladesh Electricity Bill by 40% in 2025

📅 September 15, 2025 👁️ 12,847 views ⏱️ 12 min read 💰 Save Tk500-1000/month

💡 Real Results

Mosfika Rahman from Dhanmondi reduced her monthly bill from Tk4,200 to Tk2,400 per month using these exact strategies. Their annual savings: Tk21,600!

With electricity prices rising across Bangladesh, families are struggling with monthly bills that can exceed Tk3,000-5,000. But what if I told you that you could reduce your electricity bill by 40% without sacrificing comfort?

💡 The 40% Reduction Strategy

🌡️ Air Conditioner Temperature Control

  • Set to 25°C instead of 22°C: Saves Tk300-500/month
  • Use timer function: Auto-off after 6 hours saves Tk200/month
  • Pre-cool before load shedding: Maintains comfort, saves Tk150/month
  • Clean filters monthly: Improves efficiency, saves Tk250/month

🔌 Eliminate Phantom Load

Electronics on standby still consume 5-15% of total power. Simple solution:

  • • Unplug TV, sound systems when not in use
  • • Use power strips with switches
  • • Turn off WiFi router at night
  • • Potential savings: Tk200-400/month

⏰ Peak-Hour Strategies

BPDB Time-of-Use (if applicable):

  • • Peak hours: 6-10 PM (higher rates)
  • • Run heavy appliances 10 PM - 6 AM
  • • Stay under 200 units for lowest slab
  • • Potential savings: Tk200-400/month

DESCO/DPDC customers:

  • • Avoid 6-9 PM peak hours
  • • Use dishwasher/washing machine at night
  • • Take advantage of digital payment discounts
  • • Potential savings: Tk150-300/month

🌀 Load Shedding Optimization

Use load shedding schedules to your advantage:

  • Pre-cool rooms before scheduled outages
  • Charge devices during off-peak hours
  • Cook meals during stable electricity periods
  • Use solar chargers/power banks during outages
