How accurate is this free Bangladesh electricity bill calculator 2025?

Our free BD bill calculator uses current 2025 electricity rates from BPDB, DESCO, DPDC, and WZPDCL. It provides estimates within 95% accuracy based on official tariff structures and your appliance usage patterns. Perfect for budget planning and energy cost management.

What are the current electricity unit rates in Bangladesh 2025?

Current Bangladesh electricity rates 2025 vary by utility: BPDB: ৳3.5-৳9.9 per kWh, DESCO: ৳4.5-৳8.5 per kWh, DPDC: ৳4.0-৳9.0 per kWh, WZPDCL: ৳4.2-৳8.8 per kWh. Our free calculator uses latest government-approved tariffs for accurate bill estimation.

How can I reduce my electricity bill in Bangladesh?

Use LED bulbs (save 80% energy), set AC temperature to 24-26°C, unplug devices when not in use, use energy-efficient appliances, and consider solar water heaters. Our calculator helps track savings from these changes.
