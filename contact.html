Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How quickly can you help reduce my electricity bill?

Most clients see 20-40% reduction in their electricity bills within the first month by implementing our personalized energy-saving strategies. We provide immediate actionable advice during consultation.

💰 Is the consultation service free?

Yes! Our basic energy consultation is completely free. We also offer premium energy audit services for comprehensive home analysis at affordable rates.

🏠 Do you provide on-site home energy audits?

Yes, we offer comprehensive on-site energy audits throughout Dhaka and surrounding areas. Our certified energy experts will visit your home to identify specific savings opportunities.

⚡ Which utility companies do you work with?

We provide specialized advice for all major Bangladesh electricity providers: BPDB, DESCO, DPDC, WZPDCL, NESCO, and others. Each has unique rate structures we optimize for.

🌞 Do you help with solar panel installations?

We provide solar feasibility analysis, cost-benefit calculations, and connect you with trusted solar installers. We help determine if solar is right for your specific situation and location.

📞 How fast do you respond to inquiries?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 2-4 hours during business days. For urgent energy issues or bill disputes, we offer same-day consultation scheduling.
